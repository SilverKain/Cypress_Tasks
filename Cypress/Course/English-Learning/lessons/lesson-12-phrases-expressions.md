|-----------|----------|
| Meeting someone | Nice to meet you! |
| Saying goodbye | Take care! See you! |
| Thanking | Thank you so much! |
| Apologizing | I'm sorry about that. |
| Didn't hear | Sorry, could you repeat that? |
| Don't understand | What do you mean? |
| Agreeing | Exactly! I agree. |
| Disagreeing | I see your point, but... |
| Good news | That's wonderful! |
| Bad news | I'm sorry to hear that. |
