## üìö What is a Noun? / –ß—Ç–æ —Ç–∞–∫–æ–µ —Å—É—â–µ—Å—Ç–≤–∏—Ç–µ–ª—å–Ω–æ–µ?

A **noun** is a word that names:
- **People:** teacher, doctor, Anna, friend
- **Places:** city, Russia, school, home
- **Things:** book, table, phone, car
- **Ideas:** love, happiness, education, time

### **Types of Nouns:**

| Type | Description | Examples |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Common nouns** | General names | city, dog, book |
| **Proper nouns** | Specific names (capitalized) | Moscow, John, Monday |
| **Countable nouns** | Can be counted | apple(s), car(s), idea(s) |
| **Uncountable nouns** | Cannot be counted | water, music, information |

---

## üî§ Articles: A, An, The

English has THREE articles. Russian doesn't have articles, so this is a new concept!

### **Indefinite Articles: A / AN**

Use **a** or **an** for:
- **First mention** of something
- **One of many** things
- **General statements**

| Article | When to use | Examples |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **A** | Before consonant sounds | a book, a car, a university |
| **AN** | Before vowel sounds | an apple, an hour, an umbrella |

‚ö†Ô∏è **Important:** It's about the SOUND, not the letter!
- "a university" (because "university" starts with /j/ sound)
- "an hour" (because the "h" is silent)
- "a European" (because it starts with /j/ sound)
- "an honest person" (because "h" is silent)

### **Practice: A or AN?**
1. ___ apple *(an - starts with vowel sound)*
2. ___ banana *(a - starts with consonant)*
3. ___ university *(a - starts with /j/ sound)*
4. ___ hour *(an - silent h)*
5. ___ European country *(a - starts with /j/ sound)*
6. ___ honest answer *(an - silent h)*
7. ___ one-way street *(a - starts with /w/ sound)*
8. ___ umbrella *(an - starts with vowel)*

### **Definite Article: THE**

Use **THE** when:

| Situation | Example | Explanation |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| **Specific/known thing** | Give me **the** book | The book we both know |
| **Second mention** | I have a car. **The** car is red. | Already mentioned |
| **Only one exists** | **the** sun, **the** moon, **the** internet | Unique things |
| **Superlatives** | **the** best, **the** biggest | Only one can be best |
| **With "of"** | **the** capital of Russia | Specific reference |
| **Ordinal numbers** | **the** first, **the** second | Specific order |

### **THE Pronunciation:**
- Before consonants: /√∞…ô/ - "the book" /√∞…ô b äk/
- Before vowels: /√∞i/ - "the apple" /√∞i Àà√¶p…ôl/

### **No Article (Zero Article) √ò**

DON'T use articles with:

| Category | Examples | Rule |
|----------|----------|------|
| **Proper nouns** | Moscow, John, Russia | Names of specific people/places |
| **Languages** | English, Russian | But: "the English language" |
| **Meals** | breakfast, lunch, dinner | General meals |
| **Sports/games** | football, chess | Activities |
| **Academic subjects** | mathematics, history | But: "the history of Russia" |
| **Uncountable (general)** | water, music, love | General concept |
| **Plural (general)** | Dogs are loyal. | General statements |

## üìä Articles Comparison Chart

| Situation | Example | Why |
|-----------|---------|-----|
| First mention, one of many | I saw **a** dog. | Any dog, not specific |
| Specific, known | I saw **the** dog. | The dog we talked about |
| Unique things | **The** sun is bright. | Only one sun |
| General plural | **√ò** Dogs are loyal. | Dogs in general |
| General uncountable | **√ò** Water is essential. | Water in general |
| Specific uncountable | **The** water in this glass. | Specific water |
| Before professions | She is **a** teacher. | One of many teachers |
| With proper nouns | **√ò** Moscow is beautiful. | Names don't need articles |

## üî¢ Plural Nouns / –ú–Ω–æ–∂–µ—Å—Ç–≤–µ–Ω–Ω–æ–µ —á–∏—Å–ª–æ

### **Regular Plurals: Add -S**

| Singular | Plural | Pronunciation |
|----------|--------|---------------|
| book | books | /b äks/ |
| car | cars | /k…ëÀêrz/ |
| table | tables | /Ààte…™b…ôlz/ |
| day | days | /de…™z/ |

### **Spelling Rules:**

| Ending | Rule | Example |
|--------|------|---------|
| -s, -ss, -sh, -ch, -x, -z | Add **-es** | bus ‚Üí buses, watch ‚Üí watches |
| consonant + -y | Change y to **-ies** | city ‚Üí cities, baby ‚Üí babies |
| vowel + -y | Add **-s** | day ‚Üí days, key ‚Üí keys |
| -f, -fe | Change to **-ves** | knife ‚Üí knives, life ‚Üí lives |
| -o (some words) | Add **-es** | tomato ‚Üí tomatoes, potato ‚Üí potatoes |
| -o (others) | Add **-s** | photo ‚Üí photos, piano ‚Üí pianos |

### **Irregular Plurals:**

| Singular | Plural | Note |
|----------|--------|------|
| man | men | Vowel change |
| woman | women | /Ààw…™m…™n/ |
| child | children | -en ending |
| person | people | Complete change |
| tooth | teeth | Vowel change |
| foot | feet | Vowel change |
| mouse | mice | Vowel change |
| fish | fish | Same form |

## üö´ Uncountable Nouns / –ù–µ–∏—Å—á–∏—Å–ª—è–µ–º—ã–µ —Å—É—â–µ—Å—Ç–≤–∏—Ç–µ–ª—å–Ω—ã–µ

Some nouns CANNOT be counted and have NO plural form.

### **Common Uncountable Nouns:**

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Liquids** | water, milk, juice, coffee, tea, oil |
| **Foods** | bread, rice, meat, cheese, butter, sugar |
| **Materials** | wood, paper, glass, plastic, gold, silver |
| **Abstract ideas** | love, happiness, information, advice, knowledge |
| **Weather** | rain, snow, wind, sunshine |
| **Activities** | homework, work, music, travel |

### **How to count uncountable nouns:**
Use **quantity words**:
- a **piece** of advice / information / furniture
- a **glass** of water / milk / juice
- a **cup** of coffee / tea
- a **slice** of bread / pizza
- a **bottle** of water / wine
- **some** water / advice / information

### **Wrong vs Right:**
| ‚ùå Wrong | ‚úÖ Right |
|----------|---------|
| an information | a piece of information |
| three advices | three pieces of advice |
| two breads | two slices of bread |
| many homeworks | a lot of homework |

## üí° Common Mistakes with Articles

### **Mistake 1: Using "a" with uncountable nouns**
- ‚ùå I need a water.
- ‚úÖ I need water. / I need a glass of water.

### **Mistake 2: Forgetting articles before singular countable nouns**
- ‚ùå I have car.
- ‚úÖ I have **a** car.

### **Mistake 3: Using articles with proper nouns**
- ‚ùå I live in the Moscow.
- ‚úÖ I live in Moscow.

### **Mistake 4: Using "the" for general statements**
- ‚ùå The dogs are loyal animals.
- ‚úÖ Dogs are loyal animals.

### **Mistake 5: Forgetting "the" with superlatives**
- ‚ùå It's best restaurant in city.
- ‚úÖ It's **the** best restaurant in **the** city.
